SmartETS

Examination Tracking System

Empowers education with Exam Tracking

Exam Tracking System is a sophisticated examination tracking system with cutting edge technology. The system automates the complete cycle of examination management such as examination fees management, hall ticket generation, exam halls allotments, processing of evaluation, publication of results, revaluation, arrears tracking and so on.

Exam Tracking greatly enhances the education sector to take advantage of the technology and to achieve tangible benefits in conducting exams, managing and storing exam related details.

It provides utmost security to the data, reduces a lot of man hours, enhances accuracy and meets all the requirements of the educational institution in conducting examination.

  • Results Management - enables the user to prepare and maintain all results related details such as subject wise results, results abstract, moderation process, etc.
  • Certificates Management - enables the user to generate and print all the relevant certificates for the students.
  • Reports Management - helps the user to generate various results based on examination process, results and certificates.
