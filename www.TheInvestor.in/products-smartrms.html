SmartRMS

Restaurant Management System

Managing restaurants made easy with SmartRMS

SmartRMS is an all encompassing Restaurant Management System for managing the restaurant effectively and efficiently. It simplifies the business activities at the restaurant and at the back-office. Managing the restaurant is made easy with SmartRMS. It greatly improves business profitability, user's efficiency and customer satisfaction. It is compatible with all the accessories required at the point of sale.

SmartRMS is a bespoke solution and is a boon to the hospitality industry. It is suitable to all sizes and types of restaurants starting from simple fast food to highly sophisticated restaurants. The intuitive interfaces are user-friendly, easy to learn and use.

  • Features
  • Advantages
  • Benefits
  • System Requirements
  • Flexible User Interface - A very flexible and user-friendly interface that could be customized according to the users' role and access rights.
  • Effective Tracking of Activities - Tracking of all the activities of purchase (like quotation enquiry, proforma invoice, purchase order, goods received note, payments, etc) and accounts are made very easy and efficient.
  • POS Devices - SmartRMS readily configures and functions with all the POS devices available at the restaurant like EDC machine, Cash drawer, etc.
  • Table Management - Facility to track table wise orders, pending orders and payments
  • Service Management - Facility to cater to different service types such as table service, car service, etc.
  • Shift Management - Helps you to manage the shifts of the employees and track performance.
  • Reservation Management - Helps you to manage efficiently all the reservation details.
  • Multiple Payment Modes - SmartRMS supports multiple payment modes such as cash, card and coupons.
  • Currency Conversion - Facility to accept various currencies with the ability to define their values periodically.
  • Inventory and Audits - Could be effectively carried out with SmartRMS modules.
  • Reports - Strategic reports can be generated based on sessions, items, purchase, sales, stocks, etc.
