Medication Reminder

SmartMediRem

About This Product

Medication Reminder is an Android application to remind people of the medications they need to take and at what time. This application is very much helpful not only for those people who tend to forget their medications, but to all those who are under medication, so that they don't miss out what they ought to take and get well soon.

Product Features

The application is very flexible and simple to use. The users can set their own meals time, medication details and alarms. The users will also be able to view the log of their medications and the list.

Restaurant Management System

SmartRMS

About This Product

Restaurant Management System is an Android application which is developed for the Android Tablet, which allows the managers and the waiters to allot and to take orders of the customers as well as to print the KOT.

Product Features

This application is very effective in viewing the table status, table allocation, allotting waiters for the tables, taking orders for the table, printing multiple KOTs and billing. This application runs as an add-on with the SmartRMS developed by TheInvestor.

E-Challan System

SmartCTP

About This Product

The E-Challan System is developed for the Chennai Traffic Police to centralize and to speed up the process of fining of the traffic violations. This application can be used for voice calling (replacing walky talky), anywhere fining and e-challan generation and to lodge complaint about traffic violations. The application provides for Bluetooth enabled prints of the e-challans.

Product Features

This is a centralized application which is login based not instrument based. At present, it is focused on Android Tablets and can run on all Android systems.

Point of Sale

SmartPOS

About This Product

Point of Sale application is an Android application that can be deployed in large retail stores where multiple billing points are necessary. The billing can be done then and there by the sales persons and can be printed. The bill or bill-id is given to the customers using which the customers can pay the cash at the counter.

Product Features

This application is also greatly useful for the travelling sales persons. They can take orders from the customers in different places, and the orders are instantly sent to the warehouse.
