Management Team

J.V.Sreedhar
Chairman

 

Sreedhar J. Veeramuthu is the pioneering founder and Chairman of TheInvestor Technologies Private Limited. After serving Anabond Ltd for about 26 years in various capacities, last being the Board of Director responsible for Projects / Technology and Anadocs IT Solutions - a 100% subsidiary of Anabond Ltd, he founded TheInvestor Technologies Pvt Ltd.

Tarun Kumar Jhabakh
Chief Operating Officer

 

Tarun Jhabakh has over 10 years of experience in the global information technology field. He handles the companies key functions such as corporate strategy, operations management and talent management. Tarun holds a bachelors degree in Computer Science and Engineering.

Robert Maria Joseph
Chief Executive Officer

 

Robert Maria Joseph has a successful track record of heading the software division in various firms before joining TheInvestor as Chief Executive Officer. His expertise and knowledge in running the business reflects in the growth of TheInvestor. TheInvestor greatly benefits from his strategic planning, smooth running of operations and venturing into new horizons.

Antony Nirmal Raj
Chief Technology Officer

 

Antony Nirmal Raj is a practicing architect brings in over 10 years of professional experience in Enterprise Product development & IT Management. He helps customers to leverage IT resources to meet changing business needs. Successfully lead product teams and delivered solutions. His expertise includes Enterprise Architecture, SaaS, Enterprise Java technologies, SOA, strong technical leadership, agile software development methods & System integration. He holds MBA in Systems (IT), and Bachelors in Computer Science.
